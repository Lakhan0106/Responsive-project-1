Tours Packages We Offer

"At Wander India, we cater to every traveler’s dream. Whether you’re seeking thrilling adventures, cultural immersions, or peaceful retreats, our thoughtfully designed tour packages have something for everyone. Discover the magic of India with our expert-curated packages designed to make your journey unforgettable!"

Himalayan Retreat: Delhi to Kullu

"Escape the hustle and bustle of Delhi and journey to the serene valleys of Kullu. Experience the breathtaking beauty of the Himalayas, with lush green landscapes, snow-capped peaks, and the soothing sound of the Beas River. This package includes visits to Manali, Solang Valley, and local cultural attractions, making it perfect for adventure seekers and nature lovers alike."

Coastal Bliss: Mumbai to Goa

"Embark on a scenic journey from the bustling streets of Mumbai to the sun-soaked beaches of Goa. Dive into the vibrant nightlife, indulge in Goan delicacies, and explore historic Portuguese architecture. This package blends relaxation and excitement, offering everything from beach parties to serene backwater cruises."

Southern Charm: Chennai to Kanyakumari

"Travel through the heart of Tamil Nadu, from the cultural hub of Chennai to the southernmost tip of India, Kanyakumari. Along the way, visit majestic temples, enjoy picturesque coastal drives, and witness the spectacular confluence of the Bay of Bengal, Arabian Sea, and Indian Ocean. A perfect journey for those seeking spiritual and scenic experiences."

Tea Trails: Kolkata to Darjeeling

"Embark on a picturesque journey from the bustling city of Kolkata to the tranquil hills of Darjeeling. Known as the 'Queen of the Hills,' Darjeeling offers breathtaking views of the Kanchenjunga, lush tea gardens, and colonial charm. Enjoy a ride on the historic toy train, visit monasteries, and sip world-famous Darjeeling tea as you immerse yourself in the serene beauty of the Himalayas. Perfect for nature enthusiasts and those seeking a peaceful retreat."
